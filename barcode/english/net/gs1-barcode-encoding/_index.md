## What is **create gs1 datamatrix**?
Creating a GS1 DataMatrix means encoding a GS1 Application Identifier (AI) and the associated data into a 2‑D matrix barcode. The resulting symbol can store a lot of information in a tiny footprint, making it ideal for small items, medical devices, and high‑density packaging.
